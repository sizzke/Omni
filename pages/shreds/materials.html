Overview of Materials

Shredders Enhancement
Home ShredderHub Stuff

Materials are a feature of roblox parts, changing the visual texture and physical properties of the part. These physical properties are somewhat useful for shredder armor and blades.

"For example, concrete is heavier than plastic and sinks faster in water. When you set the material of a part or terrain, Roblox simulates its physical material properties to make this behavior just work." https://create.roblox.com/docs/parts/materials

Physical Properties

Elasticity, friction, and their weights. There are a few others like density but these two properties actually affect shredders

Elasticity

100 Elasticity part showing the effects of elasticity.

The elasticity of a part determnines the bounciness of the part when contacting something with force. Armor with more elasticity will bounce blades away, helping to remove force thats aimed at the core.

Materials with the most elasticity are default, smoothplastic, and fabric.

Friction

A part with too little friction to stop sliding.

Parts with more friction are less able to slide against surfaces, which means blades should have less of it to have more penetrating force.

Armor is not recommended to use friction, but theres not much of a difference.

The material with the least friction is ice, at 0.06.

E/F Weights

Friction and elasticity weights act as a multiplier for the value. Ice has 0.02 friction but 3 friction weight, which means it has 0.06 friction.
