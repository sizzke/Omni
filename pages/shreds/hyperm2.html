99.9M Torque HYPER Motor2 Tutorial made by Shredder Refinery.

Hyper Motor2s are a discovered setting change that upgrades your shredder in 2 ways with no downsides. The name "Hyper" comes from how it is a super boosted motor2, the effect of it being that it gives motor2s a vastly increased torque stat with a second effect of also making the motor2 plate massless.

This huge increase in torque makes your shredder much more capable of clipping through terrain. The motor2s becoming fully massless is enough to sometimes massless desync your shredder from it having zero mass. This decrease in mass will lighten your shredder, which increases acceleration and reduces inertia.

The application of hyper motor2s is as simple as setting the torque of a motor2 to 99.999. Applying this to all of your redundancy can make your shredder reach around half a billion torque.

Tips

To make sure your shredder still works with the addition of these, there are a few things to follow:

If all motor2s are set to hyper between two assemblies, massless will only spread via a massless module with staging since motors below 100 torque do not apply massless. Massless Guide

Having an uneven amount of hyper motor2s on 2 parts of your core can cause some issues, this is because there will be a giant gap in torque.

It is advised to try have as many hyper motor2s as possible since the torque stacks and therefore you will have better clipping.
