Hyper Motor twos are a very simple bug to use, you simply due to a 3 liner in the code

if maxTorque < 100 then
hinge.AngularResponsiveness=tolerance * 10
hinge.ServoMaxTorque=maxTorque * 10000000

Because of these three lines it makes it so the 99.999 torque you wouldve put in orginally gets turned into 999990000. The tolerance also gets increased, increasing the amount of actually transfered torque.
