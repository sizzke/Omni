Overview of OGs

Shredders Variant
Home ShredderHub OGs
The first shredder (thats been recorded).

OGs are the first variant of shredders to be invented, created in 2020 by Exilir8 and possibly Duckless. Instead of a correctional axis lock, it uses drag as a preventative axis lock.

Base Structure

OGs are made up of 3 segments, the tail, neck, and head.

Tail

The tail of an og is where the preventative axis lock is stored and sometimes the seat.

Wing Panel Tails

An OG Tail (Wing Panel Var.).

Wing panel tails are commonly created by adding an alternatingly oriented rectangle of wing panels on all four sides of a line, welded together by tnt.
