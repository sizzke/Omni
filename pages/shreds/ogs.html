OGs

Shredders Variant
Home ShredderHub OGs
The first shredder (thats been recorded).

OGs are the first variant of shredders to be invented, created in 2020 by Exilir8 and possibly Duckless. Instead of a correctional axis lock, it uses drag as a preventative axis lock.

Base Structure

OGs are made up of 3 segments, the tail, neck, and head.

Tail

The tail of an og is where the preventative axis lock is stored and sometimes the seat.

Wing Panel Tails

An OG Tail (Wing Panel Var.).

Wing panel tails are commonly created by adding an alternatingly oriented rectangle of wing panels on all four sides of a line, welded together by tnt. It provides a preventative axis lock by using drag.

Aero Tails (tailless)

An OG Tail (Aero Var.).

Aero Tails, called tailless OGs, are a direct upgrade to wing panel tails.

Angle blocks are able to go invisible and invincible, while also being able to be material painted. This lets you apply aero properties to an unbreakable block and therefore have unbreakable drag.

This is made by just adding a few aero angle locks with the aero material to the tail in place of wings.
